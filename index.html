Snake Water Gun

Rules :
1. If you selects S and computer selects W. Snake will drink water and you will win and vice-versa.
2.If you selects S and computer selects G. Gun will kill the snake and you will lose and vice-versa.
3.If you selects W and computer selects G. Water will destroy Gun and you will win and vice-versa.


S for Snake, W for Water, G for Gun
